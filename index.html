AN EXCLUSIVE NFT COLLECITON

DevilFish NFT is a collection of unique, aquatic-themed avatars with a two-card hand as a background.NFT collectors and poker players share a big appetite for risk, that’s why DevilFish NFTs evoke the trickster spirit of many poker greats, the spirit that the name DevilFish shares.
Within the NFT collection, holders will find avatars representing The Poker Hall of Famers. Those NFTs will be ultra rare and allocated at random.

At the end of each week, all NFT holders will be automatically entered into a poker themed game with a hand that consists of their unique two-cards and any additional cards they have managed to earn through the week. The winners of these weekly tournaments will qualify for prizes including NFTs and the native token $DVLF.
